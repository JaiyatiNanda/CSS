Bing Concert Hall

The Bing Concert Hall at Stanford will play a vital role.on campus and in the local community.The halls exceptional acoustics and the state of the art technical capabilities will showcase the world's finest performers.
The hall is scheduled to open early in 2013. The concert hall will have 844 seats in an intimate vineyard style configuration,with terraced sections wrappng all the way around the sage.
